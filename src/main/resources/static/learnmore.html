ReserveMate is a powerful reservation management platform that helps businesses of all sizes efficiently handle bookings, manage customer relationships, and optimize their operations. Whether you're running a restaurant, salon, clinic, or any service-based business, our intuitive system simplifies the entire reservation process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ReserveMate suitable for my business?

ReserveMate is designed to be versatile and can be tailored to various businesses including restaurants, salons, spas, medical clinics, fitness studios, educational institutions, and more. If your business relies on appointments or reservations, ReserveMate can help streamline your operations.

How does the pricing work?

We offer a free plan for small businesses just getting started, which includes basic features. For businesses that need additional functionality, our Premium plan offers advanced features. Pricing for the Premium plan will be announced soon.

Can I integrate ReserveMate with my existing systems?

Yes, ReserveMate offers integration capabilities with popular calendar systems (Google Calendar, Outlook), payment processors, and CRM systems. Our API also allows for custom integrations with your existing software infrastructure.

Is there a mobile app available?

ReserveMate is fully responsive and works on all devices. We're currently developing dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android to provide an even more streamlined experience for businesses on the go.
